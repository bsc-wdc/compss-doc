==========================================
:opticon:`rocket` PyCOMPSs Success Stories
==========================================

PyCOMPSs is Open Source and used successfully in many research fields, like
Life sciences, Earth sciences, Quantum computing, Engineering, etc.

Our team is actively involved into improving and extending PyCOMPSs with the
objective of enabling and easing the High Performance Computing (HPC)
exploitation for researchers from all areas.

This section provides PyCOMPSs' success stories in European projects and
collaborations from may research fields:
